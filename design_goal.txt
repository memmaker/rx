Create a minimal UI Layer for roguelikes

We focus on these constraints:
 - Terminal Size: 80x25
 - Map Size: 80x23
 - 2 Bit Monochrome (no, light, medium, full)
 - max. 26 Items in inventory/dungeon levels & monsters


We want a balanced, modern version of rogue
Main Issues

- Too much RNG
  - Monster types / HP
  - Item types
- Dungeon can be unsolvable
  - Not enough food / weapons / spells
- Starving is too harsh
- No way to avoid encounters, especially early on
- Dark rooms are way too annoying
- Getting stats permanently reduced is too harsh, needs counterplay
- Gold is useless
  - Throw gold to distract monsters
  - Buy items from shops
  - Ammo for slings
